Cacio e Pepe

Description

Cacio e Pepe is a quick, simple, and delicious pasta dish. The main 2 ingredients that make up the flavor of this dish is cheese and pepper, hence its name in Italian, Cacio e Pepe.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound spaghetti
  • 6 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 teaspoons ground black pepper
  • 1 3/4 cups grated pecorino romano cheese

Steps

  1. Bring a large pot of water to boil. Once boiling, add salt and spaghetti. Cook spaghetti according to the packaging. Once cooked, reserve 1 cup of pasta water and drain spaghetti.
  2. Heat a large skillet over medium heat and add olive oil. Add garlic and black pepper and cook until fragrant.
  3. Add cooked spaghetti, pecorino romano cheese, and 1/2 cup of pasta water. Stir until cheese is melted. Add more pasta water 1 tablespoon at a time as needed to desired consistency and serve.
